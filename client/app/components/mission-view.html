Vision & Mission

In our vision, the wheel does not need to be re-invented, by creating new tools or infrastructure. A great investment has been made in the last decades to provide global, maintainable infrastructures of storage, processing, etc.

The Oil & Gas Virtual Research Environment has been created in an effort to present researchers with a social interface for sharing and discussion of information and tools useful for research in this domain, taking advantage of all the created infrastructures for a sustainable and reliable information system.

In the future, all researchers will use a virtual research environment, sharing their lab notes with their team or the world.

VRE structure

Page Description
Workflows Shared bioinformatics or biology workflow, contains an explainatory image, a description and possibly user-posted reviews.
Databases Important databases for the domain of Oil & Gas, posted and reviewed by users, same as workflows.
External Tools Suggested tools for the domain of Oil & Gas, posted and reviewed by users, same as workflows.
Local Services In this section we are going to make available a series of locally installed tools. This will enable our registered users to directly process and analize their data.
REST API Programmable access to the information stored in this research environment.
